Why ASH & HOUND

Devoted Care for Dogs Who Need More

Some dogs need medication on schedule. Some can’t be left alone due to medical conditions. Some get stressed in kennels. And some just have owners who refuse to settle for less than excellent care.

We follow complex medication schedules, manage dietary requirements, and handle everything from post-surgery recovery to senior dogs who need extra attention. We’re certified in animal behaviour and comfortable with separation anxiety, high-energy dogs, and the kind of higher needs most sitters won’t take on.

What you get is trustworthy, devoted care—someone who’ll treat your pet the way you would. Every stay is one-on-one and private. We keep to your dog’s routine, stay close when needed, and handle whatever comes up because we’re actually there—not checking in between other bookings.

You’ll get regular updates, photos, and videos. We’re not afraid to message when something noteworthy happens or something unexpected arises.

Services

In-Home Care

We offer doggy day care and 24-hour pet boarding—either at our place or yours. Every booking is private and one-on-one with your dog. Day care plus overnight stays for the full time you’re away. We don’t offer “overnights only” and leave your pet alone all day—that’s not real care. Rates start at $200 per night.

Private Boarding

Both services are private and consistent. Perfect for dogs who need extra company, medication throughout the day, or simply can’t be left alone. No other dogs, no overlapping bookings, no risk from unfamiliar animals—unless you specifically want social interaction for your pet. Just reliable, attentive care from someone your dog knows and trusts.
